Poringatchi
About the project

From August 14-16th, I participated in Hack The Planet 2015.

I recently participated in the Orange County VR fair, and they had a bunch of wonderful projects and ideas which incorporated the Leap Motion. There was a really cute game which was inspired by Nintendo's Pet game for the DS, so I wanted to create a similar experience.

In this game you're able to interact with your pet Poring using the Leap Motion. Control your pet's Health, Hunger, and Happiness attributes by playing with it, feeding it, and giving it medicine.

One of the main challenges I ran into was the incorporation of the leap motion's infrared sensor to act as a sort of video camera to capture the images of your hands. In the end, I was able to accomplish this by creating a shader that used the image captured by the sensor and projected it unto a 3d model of hands.

The game was made using Unity, Leap Motion, and the Oculus Rift, written in C#.
