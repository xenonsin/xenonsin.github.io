About the project

Tugtime is a Don't Starve Together character mod. He's a shadow that is connected to the physical world through masks. Each mask contain different effects, and represent different emotions Tugtime wants to display.

The Happy Mask makes you run faster and enables you to autoloot things around you.

The Sad Masks surrounds Tugtime with a positive Sanity aura, that boosts the sanity of nearby players. He justs wants a hug.

Here's what I was able to accomplish:

  • • Delved through the developer's existing code to develop interesting mechanics for the character.
  • • Solved networking issues with light not appearing for clients, only on hosts.
  • • Created 3 equipment with different effects.
