About the project

From november 7-9th, I participated in HackSC, which was held in USC.

Our main focus was trying to design an Oculus Rift experience similar to the flight demo, but we wanted for your head to move independently and not determine the direction of your flight. Most of our time was spent struggling with getting the character controller hooked up because we didn't have an Xbox 360 controller. This was the first time for us to try and incorporate the Oculus Rift, but with Oculus' API it was pretty easy to implement.

Here's what I was able to accomplish:

  • • Incorporated the Oculus Rift.
  • • Wrote a character control which uses a PS3 controller.
  • • Made all buildings demolishable.

challenge post page

The game was made using Unity and the Oculus Rift, written in C#. Art assets were created by Gemma using Paint Tool Sai.
